## What's the Big Deal with CAS?

Think of your CAS as a **one-stop-shop for your investment portfolio**. It's a single statement that summarizes all your holdings in mutual funds and securities held in your demat accounts. It's generated monthly for free by the National Securities Depository Limited (NSDL) or Central Depository Services (India) Limited (CDSL).

Instead of sifting through multiple statements from different fund houses and brokers, your CAS gives you a clear, comprehensive snapshot. It shows you everything: your mutual fund units, shares, ETFs, and even government securities. For anyone serious about their finances, this is a game-changer.
